Select all the ownership types you want to include in your Potential Treatable Area.

How do I select included areas?

Select stands to include based on ownership type. You can view ownership layers here to understand coverage, but the map will not update based on your selections in this step.

Selected areas will be applied in the next step, where only included stands are available.

What does this step do?

Here you define specific areas that must be considered in the analysis — based on ownership, land designation, or other attributes.

How does this affect results?

Areas included here form the foundation of what’s considered treatable by ForSys. All exclusions and further refinements are applied after this step.
