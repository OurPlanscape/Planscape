What are Co-Benefits?

These are secondary goals that help you understand the broader impact of your scenario — for example, benefits to water, wildlife, or carbon.

Are Co-Benefits used to prioritize treatments?

No, only your selected Priority Objectives influence how ForSys ranks areas for treatment.

How do Co-Benefits impact Results?

Co-benefits will support your evaluation of the best subunits to treat by showing how additional outcomes can be supported.
