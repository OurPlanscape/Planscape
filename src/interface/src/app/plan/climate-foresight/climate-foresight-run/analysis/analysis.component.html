Climate Foresight integrates current conditions (0-100 scale metrics) with a future vulnerability analysis (Povak and Manley 2024 climate analog approach) to predict resource condition trajectories under climate change.

The model provides quantitative support for four common restoration strategies:

  1. Monitor: Conditions are favorable now and in the future.
  2. Protect: Conditions are currently favorable but decline with climate change.
  3. Adapt: Conditions are currently unfavorable, but climate is not a future impediment.
  4. Transform: Conditions are currently and remain unfavorable due to climate change.
