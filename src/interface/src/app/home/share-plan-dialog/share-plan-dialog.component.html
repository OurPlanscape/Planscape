What can I do as an Owner?

Full administrative control. You can manage planning area permissions, and have unrestricted rights to create, edit, or delete content across all features.

What can I do as a Collaborator?

Management access. You can create and edit, but you do not have permission to share access with others or delete.

What can I do as a Viewer?

Read-only access to all dashboards or reports. You cannot edit, create, or delete data.
