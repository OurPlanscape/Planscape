Welcome to Planscape!

What is Planscape?

Planscape is a new wildfire resilience planning support tool designed to bring the best available state & federal data and science together to enable better land management. Planscape is for you, the regional wildland planners, to help prioritize landscape treatments. With Planscape, you can mitigate fire risk, maximize ecological benefits, and help Californiaâ€™s landscapes adapt to climate change.

Planscape includes all data from the Regional Resource Kit layers for the Sierra Nevada, Southern California, and Central Coast regions.

What can I do with Planscape?

You can view and explore the landscape resilience planning data on a set of maps. You can also visualize this data for the three regions alongside other provided data sets such as county and watershed boundaries.

Future Planscape releases aim to give you the ability to create and save planning areas, to create scenarios for various treatments, and to collaborate with other stakeholders.

Can Planscape actually help with planning?

Yes! A lot more functionality, including the ability to create, edit, save, and share plans is coming. The tool will also include integration with ForSys to allow planners to output projects that are optimized within provided constraints.

How do I do more?

Click on the Help icon on the top right corner of the tool to read the user guide or check out the Planscape Release Notes or FAQ . You can also visit Planscape.org for more information about the tool. If you would like to receive our newsletter for regular updates, you can sign up here .

How can I get started?

If you want a preview of the mapping tool, click Explore. To start creating planning areas, click Create Account.
