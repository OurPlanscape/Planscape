What is Planscape?

Planscape is a new wildfire resilience planning tool designed to bring the best available state & federal data and science together. Planscape guides regional planners in prioritizing landscape treatments to mitigate fire risk, maximize ecological benefits, and help California's landscapes adapt to climate change.


What can I do with Planscape?

This early preview of Planscape allows you to view and explore wildfire relevant data on a set of maps. Most notably, the data from the Regional Resource Kits for the Sierra Nevada region is available in the tool and can easily be visualized alongside other data sets such as county and watershed boundaries.


How do I get started?

Just click on the Sierra Nevada region button above!

Check out Planscape.org and the FAQ for more information about the tool and to sign up for regular updates as we release new versions.
