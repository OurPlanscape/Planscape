What is Planscape?

Planscape is a new wildfire resilience planning support tool designed to bring the best available state & federal data and science together to enable better land management. Planscape is for you, the regional wildland planners, to help prioritize landscape treatments. With Planscape, you can mitigate fire risk, maximize ecological benefits, and help California's landscapes adapt to climate change.

Planscape preview includes all data from the Regional Resource Kit layers for the Sierra Nevada, Southern California, and Central Coast regions.

What can I do with Planscape?

You can view and explore the landscape resilience planning data on a set of maps. You can also visualize this data for the three regions alongside other provided data sets such as county and watershed boundaries.

Future Planscape releases will give you the ability to create plans, to identify treatment areas from the plans, and to collaborate with other stakeholders.

How do I learn more?

Click on the Help icon on the top right corner of the tool to read the user guide or check out the Planscape Release Notes or FAQ . You can also visit Planscape.org for more information about the tool. If you would like to receive our newsletter for regular updates, you can sign up here .
